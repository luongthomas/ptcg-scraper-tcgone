Pikachu (Japanese: ピカチュウV PikachuV) is a Lightning-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Vivid Voltage expansion, first released in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion. Both prints feature artwork by Saki Hayashiro. In Japan, it was reprinted as one of the S-P Promotional cards with different artwork by PLANETA Tsuji and was available as part of the PikaPika! Pikachu! Campaign, which began on September 18, 2020. For every six Amazing Volt Tackle booster packs purchased in one transaction at participating stores, customers would receive a special pack containing one of five possible cards. For every Amazing Volt Tackle booster box purchased, customers would receive five special packs. The promotion ran for as long as stocks lasted. This print has the campaign logo on the right edge of the artwork.
