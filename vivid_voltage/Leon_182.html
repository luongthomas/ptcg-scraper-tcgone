Leon (Japanese: ダンデ Dande) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret card in the Vivid Voltage expansion, first released in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Ken Sugimori, while the Full Art and Secret prints feature artwork by Hideki Ishikawa.

Trivia

Origin

Leon is the Champion of the Galar Pokémon League. The interior of Wyndon Stadium is featured in the Regular print's illustration.
