Krokorok (Japanese: ワルビル Waruvile) is a Darkness-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Vivid Voltage expansion with artwork by sowsow, first released in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion. It was reprinted as one of the Japanese S-P Promotional cards with different artwork by Shin Nagasawa, made available as one of eight possible cards included in promotional packs awarded to participants of Pokémon Card Gym tournaments held during September, October, and November 2020. This print features the Pokémon Card Gym logo.

Trivia

Origin

Bite is a move in the Pokémon games that Krokorok can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Moon.
