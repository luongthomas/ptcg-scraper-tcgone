Snorlax (Japanese: カビゴン Kabigon) is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Vivid Voltage expansion with artwork by Atsuko Nishida, first released in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion. A SWSH Black Star Promotional print of this card was one of the Prerelease promos for Vivid Voltage with new artwork by Narumi Sato, available in the Vivid Voltage Build & Battle Box.

Trivia

Origin

Body Slam is a move in the Pokémon games that Snorlax can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword.
