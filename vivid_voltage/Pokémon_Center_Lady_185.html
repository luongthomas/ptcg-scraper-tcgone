Pokémon Center Lady (Japanese: ポケモンセンターのお姉さん Pokémon Center Lady) is a Supporter card. It was first released as part of the Flashfire expansion.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Flashfire expansion, first released in the Japanese Wild Blaze expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Maiko Fujiwara, while the Full Art print features artwork by Megumi Mizutani. The Regular print was reprinted in the Generations expansion, originating from the Japanese BREAK Starter Pack.

In the Sun & Moon Series, Pokémon Center Lady was reprinted in the Hidden Fates expansion with new artwork by TOKIYA, first released in the Japanese Brock of Pewter City Gym Trainer Battle Deck. In the Sword & Shield Series, it was reprinted in the Sword & Shield expansion with artwork by kirisAki, first released in the Japanese V Starter Set Grass and V Starter Set Fighting. A new Full Art print with artwork by Sanosuke Sakuma was released in Japan as one of the S-P Promotional cards and was given to customers who purchased two booster boxes of Infinity Zone upon its release at participating stores. The set logo is featured in the bottom right corner of the artwork. The Sword & Shield print was later released in the Japanese Metal Duraludon V Deck. The Sword & Shield print was reprinted again in the Champion's Path expansion. The S-P Promotional print was later included in the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Origin

Pokémon Center Nurses are characters in every core series game who restore the player's Pokémon to full health.
