Wooper (Japanese: ウパー Upah) is a Fighting-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Mud Shot is a move in the Pokémon games that Wooper can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword.
