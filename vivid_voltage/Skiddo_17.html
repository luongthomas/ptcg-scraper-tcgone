Skiddo (Japanese: メェークル Meecle) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Pokédex entry

Trivia

Origin

Synthesis and Razor Leaf are both moves in the Pokémon games that Skiddo can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
