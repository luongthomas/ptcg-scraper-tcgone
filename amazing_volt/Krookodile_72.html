Krookodile (Japanese: ワルビアル Waruvial) is a Darkness-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Tantrum is an attack that first appeared on Primeape from Jungle. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Moon.
