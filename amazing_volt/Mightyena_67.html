Mightyena (Japanese: グラエナ Graena) is a Darkness-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Ferocious Bellow is an attack that first appeared on Stoutland from Dark Explorers. Pitch-Black Fangs is an attack that first appeared on Hydreigon from Darkness Ablaze. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword.
