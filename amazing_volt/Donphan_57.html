Donphan (Japanese: ドンファン Donfan) is a Fighting-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Vivid Voltage expansion with artwork by Hasegawa Saki, first released in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion. A SWSH Black Star Promotional print of this card was one of the Prerelease promos for Vivid Voltage with new artwork by Eri Yamaki, available in the Vivid Voltage Build & Battle Box.

Origin

Earthquake is a move in the Pokémon games that Donphan can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
