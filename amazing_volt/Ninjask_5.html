Ninjask (Japanese: テッカニン Tekkanin) is a Grass-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Vivid Voltage expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Cast-Off Shell is an Ability that first appeared as a Poké-POWER on Ninjask from Legends Awakened. Absorb is a move in the Pokémon games that Ninjask can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword.
