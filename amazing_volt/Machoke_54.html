Machoke (Japanese: ゴーリキー Goriky) is a Fighting-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Champion's Path expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Low Kick is a move in the Pokémon games that Machoke can learn. Pummel is an attack that first appeared on Timburr and Gurdurr from Noble Victories. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword.
