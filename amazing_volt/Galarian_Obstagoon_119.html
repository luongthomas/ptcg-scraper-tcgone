Galarian Obstagoon (Japanese: ガラルタチフサグマ Galarian Tachifusaguma) is a Darkness-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Sword & Shield expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Sword & Shield expansion, first released in the Japanese Shield expansion. A Non Holofoil version was included in the Sword & Shield Build & Battle Box.

A Cosmos Holofoil version of this card with the Sword & Shield expansion logo in the bottom right corner of the artwork was given away in various countries to celebrate the launch of the set on February 7, 2020. In the United Kingdom, it was given to customers who purchased at least £10 worth of Pokémon Trading Card Game products at participating WHSmith high street stores[1]. In Belgium and the Netherlands, it was given to customers who purchased at least €10 worth of Sword & Shield-related TCG products at participating Game Mania stores. In France, it was given to customers who purchased at least €20 worth of TCG products at participating PicWicToys stores. In Germany, it was given to customers who purchased at least €10 worth of TCG products at participating MyToys, Smyths, and Müller stores. In Ireland, it was given to customers who purchased at least €10 worth of TCG products at participating Eason stores. In Italy, it was given to customers who purchased at least €9.99 worth of TCG products at participating GameStop, Prénatal, and Toys Center stores. In Spain, it was given to customers who purchased at least €10 worth of TCG products at participating stores. In Chile, this card and a Jumbo Snorlax V were included with any TCG purchase made from Big Bang Entertainment between August 5 and 31, 2020.

It was reprinted as a Full Art Secret card in the Vivid Voltage expansion with different artwork by aky CG Works, first released in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion. It was reprinted again as one of the SWSH Black Star Promos with different artwork by Hasuno, available in the Champion's Path Pin Collection—Spikemuth Gym released November 13, 2020.

Trivia

Origin

Obstruct is a move in the Pokémon games that Obstagoon can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Shield. The Vivid Voltage print features the Shiny version of Obstagoon.
