Galarian Meowth (Japanese: ガラルニャース Galarian Nyarth) is a Metal-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Rebel Clash expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Rebel Clash expansion, first released in the Japanese Zacian + Zamazenta Box. It was reprinted, alongside a new print with artwork by Shigenori Negishi featuring the Shiny version of Galarian Meowth, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card features the Galarian regional form of Meowth. Scratch is a move in the Pokémon games that Galarian Meowth can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Galarian Meowth.
