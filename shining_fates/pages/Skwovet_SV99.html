Skwovet (Japanese: ホシガリス Hoshigarisu) is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Rebel Clash expansion.

Found throughout the Galar region, this Pokémon becomes uneasy if its cheeks are ever completely empty of berries.

Release information

This card was included in the Rebel Clash expansion, first released in the Japanese Rebellion Crash expansion. It was reprinted, alongside a new print with artwork by Naoki Saito featuring the Shiny version of Skwovet, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Tackle and Bite are both moves in the Pokémon games that Skwovet can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Skwovet.
