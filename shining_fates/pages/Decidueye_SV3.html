Decidueye (Japanese: ジュナイパー Junaiper) is a Grass-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Darkness Ablaze expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Darkness Ablaze expansion with artwork by Hasuno, first released in the Japanese Rebellion Crash expansion. A SWSH Black Star Promotional print of this card was one of the Prerelease promos for Darkness Ablaze with new artwork by Souichirou Gunjima, available in the Darkness Ablaze Build & Battle Box. A variation of this print with a gold foil "Staff" stamp was also distributed to approved locations in the Prerelease Program. As the 2020 Championship Series was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic, locations that were eligible for Rebel Clash Prereleases retained their eligibility for Darkness Ablaze Prereleases. This print, alongside a new print with artwork by nagimiso featuring the Shiny version of Decidueye, was included in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Darkness Ablaze and Shiny prints were later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates Shiny Vault print features the Shiny version of Decidueye.
