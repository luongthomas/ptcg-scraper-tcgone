Cinderace (Japanese: エースバーンV AceburnV) is a Fire-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as one of the SWSH Black Star Promos.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Japanese VMAX Rising subset. Both prints feature artwork by 5ban Graphics. It was later included in the Galar Partners Tins as one of the SWSH Black Star Promos with different artwork by the same artist, released on February 27, 2020. Both VMAX Rising prints were later released in English in the Rebel Clash expansion. A Jumbo variant of the SWSH Promotional print was included in the Cinderace V Galar Partner Box available from Walmart in late April 2020. The Rebel Clash print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.
