Rusted Shield (Shining Fates 61)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.
Jump to: navigation, search
Trainer Item
Rusted Shield
くちたたて Rusted Shield

Illus. 5ban Graphics
English expansion Shining Fates
Rarity
English card no. 061/072
Japanese expansion Shiny Star V
Japanese card no. 169/190

Rusted Shield (Japanese: くちたたて Rusted Shield) is a Pokémon Tool card. It is part of the Shining Fates expansion.

Card text

All prints
You can't have more than 1 ACE SPEC card in your deck.
Team Plasma
Team Plasma
You can't have more than 1 ACE SPEC card in your deck.
FLARE
You can't have more than 1 card with the same name in your deck. If a card would go to the discard pile, put it in the Lost Zone instead.
TAG TEAM
Attach a Pokémon Tool to 1 of your Pokémon that doesn't already have a Pokémon Tool attached.
The Zamazenta V this card is attached to gets +70 HP.
Do Nothing  
At the beginning of your turn, flip a coin. If heads, you can do nothing during your turn. If tails, your opponent can do nothing during his or her next turn.
Do Nothing  
At the beginning of your turn, flip a coin. If heads, you can do nothing during your turn. If tails, your opponent can do nothing during his or her next turn.
You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.


Trivia

Origin

The Rusted Shield is a held item introduced in Generation VIII that allows Zamazenta to change into its Crowned Shield form. Its Hero of Many Battles form is featured in the illustration.
