Morpeko (Japanese: モルペコV MorpekoV) is a Lightning-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as part of the Sword & Shield expansion.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Sword & Shield expansion, first released in the Japanese Shield expansion. Both prints feature artwork by 5ban Graphics. It was reprinted as one of the SWSH Black Star Promos with different artwork by the same artist and was available in both the Champion's Path Premium Collection—Marnie and Champion's Path Special Collection—Marnie from October 23, 2020. The Regular Sword & Shield print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card features Morpeko in its Full Belly Mode. Spark is a move in the Pokémon games that Morpeko can learn.
