Dracovish (Japanese: ウオノラゴン Uonoragon) is a Water-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Darkness Ablaze expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Darkness Ablaze expansion, first released in the Japanese Infinity Zone expansion. It was reprinted, alongside a new print with artwork by SATOSHI NAKAI featuring the Shiny version of Dracovish, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Dracovish.
