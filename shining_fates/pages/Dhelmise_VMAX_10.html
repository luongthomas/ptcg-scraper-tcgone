Dhelmise (Japanese: ダダリンVMAX DadarinVMAX) is a Grass-type VMAX Pokémon VMAX card. It is part of the Shining Fates expansion.

Card text

Swinging Chain
ブンブンチェーン
This attack does 30 damage to 1 of your opponent's Pokémon for each Energy attached to this Pokémon. (Don't apply Weakness and Resistance for Benched Pokémon.)
Max Anchor
ダイアンカー 		240
During your next turn, this Pokémon can't use Max Anchor.
When your Pokémon VMAX is Knocked Out, your opponent takes 3 Prize cards.
