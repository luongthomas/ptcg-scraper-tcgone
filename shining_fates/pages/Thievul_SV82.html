Thievul (Japanese: フォクスライ Foxly) is a Darkness-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Sword & Shield expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Sword & Shield expansion, first released in the Japanese Sword expansion. It was reprinted, alongside a new print with artwork by Kagemaru Himeno featuring the Shiny version of Thievul, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Thief is a move in the Pokémon games that Thievul can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Thievul.
