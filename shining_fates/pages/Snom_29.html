Snom (Japanese: ユキハミ Yukihami) is a Water-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Shining Fates expansion.

Card text

Call for Family
なかまをよぶ
Search your deck for a Basic Pokémon and put it onto your Bench. Then, shuffle your deck.


Pokédex data

Snom - Worm Pokémon
No. Height Weight
872 1' (0.3 m) 8.4 lbs. (3.8 kg)
Pokédex entry
It spits out thread imbued with a frigid sort of energy and uses it to tie its body to branches, disguising itself as an icicle while it sleeps.
冷気の　混ざった　糸を　吐く。　糸で　体を　枝に　巻きつけ　氷柱の　振りをして　眠るのだ。

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Shiny card in the Shining Fates expansion, first released in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Regular print features artwork by Tika Matsuno, while the Shiny Vault print features artwork by Yumi.

Trivia

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shiny Vault print features the Shiny version of Snom.
