Drizzile (Japanese: ジメレオン Jimereon) is a Water-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Sword & Shield expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Sword & Shield expansion, first released in the Japanese Sword expansion. It was reprinted, alongside a new print with artwork by Ryuta Fuse featuring the Shiny version of Drizzile, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Drizzile.
