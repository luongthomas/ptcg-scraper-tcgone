Morgrem (Japanese: ギモー Gimoh) is a Darkness-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Rebel Clash expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Rebel Clash expansion, first released in the Japanese VMAX Rising subset. It was reprinted in the Japanese Darkness Galarian Slowbro V Deck. It was reprinted again, alongside a new print with artwork by SATOSHI NAKAI featuring the Shiny version of Morgrem, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Bite and False Surrender are both moves in the Pokémon games that Morgrem can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Morgrem.
