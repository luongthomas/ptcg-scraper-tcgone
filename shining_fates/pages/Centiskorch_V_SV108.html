Centiskorch (Japanese: マルヤクデV MaruyakudeV) is a Fire-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as part of the Darkness Ablaze expansion.

Card text

Radiating Heat
ねつほうしゃ 		20
You may discard an Energy from this Pokémon. If you do, discard an Energy attached to your opponent's Active Pokémon.
Burning Train
バーニングトレイン 		180
When your Pokémon V is Knocked Out, your opponent takes 2 Prize cards.


Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Darkness Ablaze expansion, first released in the Japanese Explosive Walker subset. Both prints feature artwork by 5ban Graphics. The Regular print was reprinted in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset, as was the Full Art print, which features the Shiny version of Centiskorch. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.
