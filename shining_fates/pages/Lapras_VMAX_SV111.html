Lapras (Japanese: ラプラスVMAX LaplaceVMAX) is a Water-type VMAX Pokémon VMAX card. It was first released as part of the Sword & Shield expansion.

Card text

G-Max Pump
キョダイポンプ 		90+
This attack does 30 more damage for each Energy attached to this Pokémon.
When your Pokémon VMAX is Knocked Out, your opponent takes 3 Prize cards.


Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Secret Rainbow Rare in the Sword & Shield expansion, first released in the Japanese Sword expansion. Both prints feature artwork by 5ban Graphics. The Regular print was reprinted in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset, as was the Secret print, which features the Shiny version of Gigantamax Lapras. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion.

Origin
