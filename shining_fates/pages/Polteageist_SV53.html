Polteageist (Japanese: ポットデス Potdeath) is a Psychic-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Darkness Ablaze expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Darkness Ablaze expansion, first released in the Japanese Explosive Walker subset. It was reprinted, alongside a new print with artwork by Sanosuke Sakuma featuring the Shiny version of Polteageist, in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. The Shiny print was later included in the Shining Fates expansion. It was reprinted as one of the SWSH Black Star Promos with different artwork by Misa Tsutsui and was available in the Shining Fates Mad Party Pin Collection—Polteageist from February 19, 2021.

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Polteageist.
