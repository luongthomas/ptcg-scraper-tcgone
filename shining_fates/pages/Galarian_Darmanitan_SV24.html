Galarian Darmanitan (Japanese: ガラルヒヒダルマ Galarian Hihidaruma) is a Water-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Rebel Clash expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Rebel Clash expansion with artwork by Kouki Saitou, first released in the Japanese Rebellion Crash expansion. It was reprinted in the Darkness Ablaze expansion with different artwork by Masakazu Fukuda. An exclusive Cracked Ice Holofoil and Non Holofoil of this print was also included in the Galarian Darmanitan Theme Deck. This print was later included in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset. A new print with artwork by nagimiso featuring the Shiny version of Galarian Darmanitan was included in the Shining Fates expansion, which also saw release in the Japanese Shiny Star V subset.

Trivia

Origin

This card features the Galarian regional form of Darmanitan. Blizzard is a move in the Pokémon games that Galarian Darmanitan can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Sword. The Shining Fates print features the Shiny version of Galarian Darmanitan.
