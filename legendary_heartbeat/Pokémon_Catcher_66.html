Pokémon Catcher (Japanese: ポケモンキャッチャー Pokémon Catcher) is an Item card. It was first released as part of the Emerging Powers expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Emerging Powers expansion, first released in the Japanese White Collection. In Japan, it was reprinted in the Battle Strength Decks, the Battle Theme Deck: Victini, the Reshiram-EX and Zekrom-EX Battle Strength Decks, and the Battle Gift Set: Thundurus vs Tornadus. It was also planned to be awarded as a Mirror Holofoil BW-P Promotional card at Pokémon World Championships 2011 Japan Representative Tournaments, which were scheduled to be held in eleven cities across Japan beginning April 3, 2011. Due to the Tōhoku earthquake and tsunami, these events were canceled and the card was never released. It was later included in the Dark Explorers expansion as an Ultra-Rare Secret card, first released in the Japanese Dark Rush expansion. The Emerging Powers print was subsequently reprinted in Japan as part of the Master Deck Build Box EX, the Everyone's Exciting Battle collection, the Blastoise + Kyurem-EX Combo Deck, and the Mewtwo vs Genesect Deck Kit.

Pokémon Catcher was reprinted during the XY Series in the Kalos Starter Set, first released in the Japanese XY Beginning Set, with new artwork by 5ban Graphics and the updated text to coincide with the XY Series errata. This artwork was later reprinted in the Japanese Xerneas and Yveltal Half Decks, the Hyper Metal Chain Deck, the Super Legend Set: Xerneas-EX & Yveltal-EX, the M Master Deck Build Box Power Style and M Master Deck Build Box Speed Style, the Golduck BREAK + Palkia-EX Combo Deck, as well as the English XY Trainer Kit. An unnumbered XY-P Promotional print of this artwork was one of five Trainer cards available in conjunction with the Pokémon Card Game Deck Building Seminar event held as part of the Pokémon Center Pokémon Card Game School. This was present at participating Pokémon Centers in Japan from October 25 to November 16, 2014 and was open to elementary school-aged children. A 20-card set comprising two copies each of five Trainer cards plus 10 Energy cards could be purchased at Pokémon Centers during the event for ¥100. It was reprinted again in the BREAKpoint expansion, first released in the Japanese Emboar-EX vs Togekiss-EX Deck Kit, and later in the Japanese BREAK Starter Pack, as well as the simultaneously released Japanese Zygarde-EX Perfect Battle Deck and M Audino-EX Mega Battle Decks. Another unnumbered XY-P Promotional print was 1 of 9 cards available in special packs sent to Japanese Event Organizers in July 2016 for distribution in official events hosted by them. This print features the Event Organizer logo in the bottom left corner of the illustration.

A new print of Pokémon Catcher with artwork by Toyste Beach was available during the Sun & Moon Series, appearing in the Sun & Moon expansion, first released in the Japanese Sun & Moon Starter Set. It was reprinted again in the Japanese Tapu Bulu-GX Enhanced Starter Set and Ash vs Team Rocket Deck Kit. A SM-P Promotional print was 1 of 9 possible cards included in special packs sent to Japanese Event Organizers in August 2017 for distribution in official events hosted by them. This print features the Event Organizer logo in the bottom right corner of the illustration. It was reprinted in the English Shining Legends expansion and in the Japanese Shining Legends subset, Solgaleo-GX & Lunala-GX Legendary Starter Set, the Ultra Sun, Forbidden Light, and Sky-Splitting Charisma expansions, the Thunderclap Spark subset, in each of the GX Starter Decks, the Super-Burst Impact expansion, and in both Trainer Battle Decks.

Pokémon Catcher was reprinted again during the Sword & Shield Series as part of the Sword & Shield expansion, first released in each of the Japanese V Starter Sets. This print has new artwork by Ryo Ueda. In Japan, the Sword & Shield print was reprinted in the Sword & Shield Premium Trainer Box, in both VMAX Starter Sets, in each of the V Starter Decks, and in the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Trivia

  • At the start of the XY Series, Pokémon Catcher was given errata that required a successful coin flip for the effect of the card to occur.
  • This card's Black & White Series effect matches that of Gust of Wind, a card previously released in the Base Set. The XY Series effect matches that of Pokémon Reversal.
