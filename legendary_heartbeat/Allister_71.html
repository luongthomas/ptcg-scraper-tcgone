Allister (Japanese: オニオン Onion) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret card in the Japanese Legendary Heartbeat subset. The Regular print features artwork by take, while the Full Art and Secret prints feature artwork by Sanosuke Sakuma.

Trivia

Origin

Allister is a Gym Leader in Galar. The interior of the Stow-on-Side Stadium is featured in the Regular print's illustration.
