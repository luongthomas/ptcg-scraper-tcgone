Beauty (Japanese: おとなのおねえさん Grown-Up Woman) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret card in the Japanese Legendary Heartbeat subset. All prints feature artwork by kirisAki.

Trivia

Origin

Beauty is a class of Trainer in the Pokémon games. This card is spelled as おとなのおねえさん instead of 大人のおねえさん as in the games. Wyndon's shopping district is featured in the Regular print's illustration.
