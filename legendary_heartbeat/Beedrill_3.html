Beedrill (Japanese: スピアー Spear) is a Grass-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Trivia

Origin

Elusive Master is an Ability that first appeared on SM Black Star Promo Greninja-GX. Sharp Sting is an attack that first appeared on Beedrill from Crimson Invasion. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
