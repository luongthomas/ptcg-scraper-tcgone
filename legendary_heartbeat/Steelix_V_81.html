Steelix (Japanese: ハガネールV HaganeilV) is a Metal-type Basic Pokémon V card. It is part of the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Card text

Raging Hammer
レイジングハンマー 		30+
This attack does 10 more damage for each damage counter on this Pokémon.
Iron Tackle
アイアンタックル 		210
This Pokémon also does 30 damage to itself.
When your Pokémon V is Knocked Out, your opponent takes 2 Prize cards.


Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Japanese Legendary Heartbeat subset. Both prints feature artwork by Satoshi Shirai.

Trivia

Origin

Raging Hammer is an attack that first appeared on Regigigas-EX from Next Destinies. Iron Tackle is an attack that first appeared on Wormadam from Secret Wonders.
