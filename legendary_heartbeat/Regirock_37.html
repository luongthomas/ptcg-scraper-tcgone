Regirock (Japanese: レジロック Regirock) is a Fighting-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Trivia

Origin

Rock Tumble is an attack that first appeared on Graveler from Neo Revelation. Megaton Fall is an attack that first appeared on Golem-EX from Generations. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
