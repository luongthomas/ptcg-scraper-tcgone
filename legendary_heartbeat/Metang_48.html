Metang (Japanese: メタング Metang) is a Metal-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Trivia

Origin

Metal Claw is a move in the Pokémon games that Metang can learn. Magnetic Blast is an attack that first appeared on Metang δ and Magnemite from EX Delta Species. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Moon.
