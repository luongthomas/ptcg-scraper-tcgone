Rocky Helmet (Japanese: ゴツゴツメット Rugged Helmet) is a Pokémon Tool card. It was first released as part of the Noble Victories expansion.

Release information

This card was first printed in the Noble Victories expansion in English and the Red Collection in Japanese. It was later reprinted as both a Regular and an Ultra-Rare Secret card in the English Boundaries Crossed expansion, each with different artwork by Ryo Ueda. The Regular print originates from the Japanese Keldeo Battle Strength Deck, where it was a Holofoil card; the Secret print originates from the Japanese Freeze Bolt expansion. The original print was later released in Japan as part of the Master Deck Build Box EX, and again in the Mewtwo vs Genesect Deck Kit. Rocky Helmet was reprinted again during the Sword & Shield Era with new artwork by Studio Bora Inc. This print was included in the Legendary Heartbeat subset.

Trivia

Origin

The Rocky Helmet is a held item introduced in Pokémon Black and White. It damages an attacker who makes contact with the holder.
