Expedition Set (Silver Lance 60)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.
Jump to navigationJump to search
This article is about a Japanese Pokémon Trading Card Game card which has not yet been officially released in English and, hence, may not be released outside of Japan. As such, this article may contain translated Japanese terms instead of English terms.
Trainer Item
Expedition Set
サバイバルセット Survival Set

Illus. AYUMI ODASHIMA
Japanese expansion Silver Lance
Japanese Rarity
Japanese card no. 060/070

Expedition Set (Japanese: サバイバルセット Survival Set) is an Item card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Card text

All prints
You can't have more than 1 ACE SPEC card in your deck.
Team Plasma
Team Plasma
You can't have more than 1 ACE SPEC card in your deck.
FLARE
You can't have more than 1 card with the same name in your deck. If a card would go to the discard pile, put it in the Lost Zone instead.
TAG TEAM
{{{ruletext}}}
Look at the bottom 3 cards of your deck and put them on top of your deck in any order.
Do Nothing  
At the beginning of your turn, flip a coin. If heads, you can do nothing during your turn. If tails, your opponent can do nothing during his or her next turn.
Do Nothing  
At the beginning of your turn, flip a coin. If heads, you can do nothing during your turn. If tails, your opponent can do nothing during his or her next turn.
You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.


Trivia

Origin

These are a set of clothes given to the player by Peony as part of the story in The Crown Tundra Pokémon Sword and Shield Expansion Pass.
