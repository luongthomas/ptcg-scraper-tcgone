Walrein (Japanese: トドゼルガ Todoseruga) is a Water-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Aurora Beam is a move in the Pokémon games that Walrein can learn. Hail Prison is an attack that first appeared on Kyurem from Unbroken Bonds. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
