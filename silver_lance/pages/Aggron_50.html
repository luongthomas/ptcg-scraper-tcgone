Aggron (Japanese: ボスゴドラ Bossgodora) is a Metal-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Card text

Guard Press
ガードプレス 		100
During your opponent's next turn, this Pokémon takes 30 less damage from attacks (after applying Weakness and Resistance).
Earth Break
ちきゅうわり 		240
This attack also does 30 damage to each of your Pokémon. (Don't apply Weakness and Resistance for Benched Pokémon.)

Trivia

Origin

Guard Press is an attack that first appeared on Steelix from Unleashed. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
