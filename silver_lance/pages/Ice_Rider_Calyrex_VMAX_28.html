Ice Rider Calyrex (Japanese: はくばバドレックスVMAX White Horse BudrexVMAX) is a Water-type VMAX Pokémon VMAX card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Card text

Emperor Ride
エンペラーライド 		10+
This attack does 30 more damage for each of your opponent's Benched Pokémon.
Max Lance
ダイランス 		10+
You may discard up to 2 Energy from this Pokémon. If you do, this attack does 120 more damage for each card you discarded in this way.
When your Pokémon VMAX is Knocked Out, your opponent takes 3 Prize cards.


Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Secret Rainbow Rare, and a Special Full Art card in the Japanese Silver Lance expansion. The Regular and Rainbow Rare prints feature artwork by 5ban Graphics, while the Special Full Art print features artwork by Oswaldo KATO.

Origin
