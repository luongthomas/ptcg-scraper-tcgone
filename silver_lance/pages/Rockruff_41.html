Rockruff (Japanese: イワンコ Iwanko) is a Fighting-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Crunch

Trivia

Origin

Crunch is a move in the Pokémon games that Rockruff can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Moon.
