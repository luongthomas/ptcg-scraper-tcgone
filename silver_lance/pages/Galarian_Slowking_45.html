Galarian Slowking (Japanese: ガラルヤドキング Galarian Yadoking) is a Darkness-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card features the Galarian regional form of Slowking. Spray Fluid is an attack that first appeared on Stunky from Flashfire. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Shield.
