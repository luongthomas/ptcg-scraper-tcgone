Doctor (Japanese: ドクター Doctor) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret Rainbow Rare in the Japanese Silver Lance expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Megumi Mizutani, while the Full Art and Rainbow Rare prints feature artwork by Sanosuke Sakuma.
