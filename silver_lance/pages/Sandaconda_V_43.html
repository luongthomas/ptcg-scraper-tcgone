Sandaconda (Japanese: サダイジャV SadaijaV) is a Fighting-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as part of the Silver Lance expansion.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Japanese Silver Lance expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Shin Nagasawa, while the Full Art print features artwork by PLANETA Tsuji. In Japan, it was reprinted as a Full Art S-P Promotional card with different artwork by Narumi Sato and was given to customers who purchased at least two booster boxes of Silver Lance upon its release at participating stores. The set logo is featured on the right side of the artwork.

Trivia

Origin

Wall of Sand is an Ability that first appeared on Palossand from Sun & Moon. Land Crush is an attack that first appeared on Brock's Golem from Gym Heroes, though it was incorrectly translated as Fissure.
